About the Creator & Project

Hi! I'm Son, a student currently living and studying in Vietnam. Solved / Advanced is my very first personal web development project, built out of my passion for speedcubing.

I created this website to give back to the speedcubing community by collecting, comparing, and curating the absolute fastest OLL/PLL algorithms from many different databases into a single, clean workspace. This helps cubers easily find the most optimal move sequences without browsing multiple sites. I also hope to generate a small passive income as an allowance through AdSense to support my studies and keep this site running.

I hope you enjoy training with it! Feel free to suggest any improvements or report any incorrect notations.
